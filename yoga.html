Get Started


Want to start a yoga practice? Yoga is a great form of exercise that builds body strength, flexibility and helps with your mood. What you need to get started is a yoga mat and blocks or a pillow. These aren't absolutely necessary though you can get started without them.



Other Resources


To busy to go to a local studio and take classes? No problem, stay home and pull out your mat and laptop or phone. There are a ton of great resources online, and videos on YouTube to follow along with. Start your practice right at home! With videos and online instructors you can take classes anywhere from beginner to advanced experiences.
